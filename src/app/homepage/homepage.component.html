Our Sections

St. Aloysius Convent School has a Primary Section and Pre-Primary Section apart from the High School Section. The High School section is further divided into the English medium and Semi English Medium. Both the Medium’s are Government aided. The Primary Section has an Aided Marathi/Semi Medium and a Non Aided English Medium.
